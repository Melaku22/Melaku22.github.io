A Poem

"The Subtle"

By Melaku Belay

The Subtle

A certain silent sophistication.

Mastery perfected

A mystery to the senses, the ever illusive

A moving stillness all too swift, as the deliberate quiet becomes its canvas..

The Subtle, an allure that lures the curious mind..

Its very subtleties overlooked and the eyes begin to vainly chase what they cannot see..

In time they shall succumb to its slow and sweet song,

While the mind is left to perceive the imperceptible...
