My Art

If Only

If only the water was closer where it may douse the fire... If only the fire could bring heat to where all warmth has expired...

Passion-Purpose-Pain

The Fight of the Light

The light always makes its greatest stand; its greatest fight, right before the sun sets. Right from our eyes' grip, brilliant colors slowly drift away. Till there is nothing left to see, but only to hear. As the darkness begins to sing..

The Darkest Night

So much in the dark. Emotions loudly paint the night.. Untethered, they make their way.. Slowly.. deliberately...But only in the Dark's glow...

Take Flight

With the subtle rising and falling of the chest. From the steady flow of the breath. Dreams and thoughts take flight, but unlike the Sun shall never set..

Still shot of the soul

Faint colors coalesce as the wind carries them on. To drift away until all their meaning is lost. Leaving what only truly needs to be seen. In that brief moment, all is made bare. A mosaic from what's left...A still shot of the soul...
